Return & Refund Policy

1. Applicability

Policy applies when you place order on AJIO. AJIO can update this policy anytime—changes are effective going forward :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}.

2. Cancellation

  • Cancellations allowed before the order is packed/invoice generated.
  • AJIO initiates refund within 2 business days after cancellation request :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}.
  • If already packed/shipped, you can reject the delivery to return the order :contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}.

3. Returns

  • Return window for non-electronics: generally 10 days; electronics: up to 7 days (see Annexure A) :contentReference[oaicite:4]{index=4}.
  • Products eligible for returns include damaged, defective, wrong item, poor quality, size/fit/colour dissatisfaction :contentReference[oaicite:5]{index=5}.
  • Must return items unused, with tags, packaging intact, original invoice, accessories :contentReference[oaicite:6]{index=6}.
  • Certain hygiene or electronic items are non-returnable (e.g., innerwear, cosmetics, fragrances, electronics unless defective) :contentReference[oaicite:7]{index=7}.
  • Bundled packages must be returned entirely—not partly :contentReference[oaicite:8]{index=8}.

4. Refund Process

  • AJIO processes refunds within 7–10 business days after receiving returned products and verifying condition :contentReference[oaicite:9]{index=9}.
  • Refund to original payment method or AJIO Wallet/store credit; wallet refunds can’t be transferred :contentReference[oaicite:10]{index=10}.
  • Shipping charges and convenience fee not refunded unless product defect is proved and claimed within 7 days :contentReference[oaicite:11]{index=11}.
  • AJIO sends updates via email/SMS; banks may take extra time :contentReference[oaicite:12]{index=12}.

5. Exchange

Exchange allowed for size/fit issues (reference product page for timelines). Same conditions as return and subject to availability :contentReference[oaicite:13]{index=13}.

6. Pick-up & Logistics

AJIO arranges reverse logistics pick-up. If unavailable, user must self-ship to AJIO warehouse and email courier receipt. In such cases, AJIO may credit ₹150 store credits after quality check :contentReference[oaicite:14]{index=14}.

7. Customer Support

For help, write to customercare@ajio.com or use the self-care section on AJIO website :contentReference[oaicite:15]{index=15}.
